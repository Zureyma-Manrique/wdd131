MindWell Hub

Your Companion for Mental Well-being

1. Site Name

MindWell Hub

Why this name: "MindWell Hub" combines the concepts of mental wellness and being a central resource hub. The name is memorable, friendly, and clearly communicates the site's focus on mental health and well-being. It suggests a safe, supportive space where users can find comprehensive resources for their mental health journey.

Optional domain: mindwell-hub.org

2. Site Purpose

MindWell Hub serves as a comprehensive digital resource center dedicated to promoting mental health awareness and providing accessible self-care tools. The site offers evidence-based information about mental well-being concepts, curated external resources from reputable organizations, and interactive self-care tools including mindfulness exercises and a mental well-being questionnaire. Our mission is to make mental health resources more accessible and to empower users with practical tools for maintaining their psychological well-being.

3. Scenarios

Scenario 1: "I've been feeling overwhelmed lately and want to understand if what I'm experiencing is normal. Are there some quick self-assessment tools or questionnaires that might help me better understand my current mental state?"
Scenario 2: "I know I need to practice better self-care, but I don't know where to start. What are some simple, practical techniques I can try today to help manage my stress and improve my mental well-being?"
Scenario 3: "I want to learn more about mental health resources available in my area or online. Where can I find trusted helplines, support groups, or professional services?"

Color Rationale: These colors were chosen for their calming, therapeutic qualities. The teal blue primary color evokes trust and tranquility, while the soft purple secondary color adds warmth and creativity. The sage green accent provides a natural, healing feel. Together, they create a soothing palette that supports the mental health theme.

5. Typography

Georgia Serif Font

Usage: Main headings (H1, H2) and logo text

Rationale: Georgia provides excellent readability and a professional, trustworthy appearance that's important for health-related content.

Segoe UI Sans-Serif Font

Usage: Body text, navigation, buttons, and form elements

Rationale: Segoe UI is highly legible on screens and creates a clean, modern appearance that's easy to read for extended periods.
